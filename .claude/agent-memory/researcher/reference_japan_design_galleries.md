## Primary Japanese Design Gallery Sites

- **MUUUUU.ORG** — https://muuuuu.org/category/industry/restaurant — Best daily-updated gallery of excellent Japanese websites. Restaurant category has 50+ curated entries. Use as first stop for any Japan restaurant design research.
- **Web Design Clip** — https://webdesignclip.com/category/eat-drink/ — Curated JP food/restaurant/cafe sites. Shows CMS platform used (WordPress, Studio, Wix) for each site.
- **BRIK Gallery** — https://brik.co.jp/tips/5813 — Feature articles on food/restaurant site design with editorial analysis.
- **Web幹事** — https://web-kanji.com/posts/restaurant-homepage-design — Maintained list with 2026-edition updates. Good for broad overview.

## International Awards — Japan Restaurant Entries
- Awwwards Japan winner list: https://www.awwwards.com/winner-list/Japan
- Only a handful of Japanese restaurant sites have won Awwwards: 24-Sekki (Sendai, Honorable Mention), Harajuku Dacci Pasta Labo (Nominee)
- Restaurant & Bar Design Awards: https://restaurantandbardesignawards.com/ — Interior focused, not web

## Research Tip
Japanese gallery sites use katakana/romaji restaurant names inconsistently. Search both Japanese (高級レストラン ウェブデザイン) and English (high end restaurant website japan) to avoid missing sites.
